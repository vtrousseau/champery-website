Picturesque destination, summer and Winter alike!

You like skiing, hiking, mountain biking? You rather enjoy relaxing thermal baths, the quietness and authenticity of a mountain village? It’s all there, including an outstanding view from the beautiful balcony and a cosy apartment fully refurbished!

THE APARTMENT

Ideally located, modern and cozy, completely renovated, with all the necessary equipment, it enjoys a breathtaking view and a quiet rest.

THE VILLAGE

Charm and authenticity are Champéry’s main assets, which can be explored through the free train that criss-crosses the village and its surroundings.

SKIING

With more than 400 miles of pistes, skiing is #1 activity within “les Portes du Soleil” (the doors to the sun), an international ski area that combines 10 ski resorts and makes it one of Switzerland’s most attractive place for skiers and snowboarders. Nordic skiing or snowshoe trekking is another popular activity, whether you’re an expert or a beginner.

HIKING

If you enjoy hiking, you’ll discover spectacular landscapes, gather blackberries or mushrooms, and watch the alpine fauna and flora through the numerous hiking trails available. If you’re after a thrilling experience, swoop down the slopes on a mountain bike tracks or a scooter!

RELAXING

Treat yourself with a thermal waters bath, a massage, sauna or hammam in Val d’Illiez’s thermal centre or enjoy a spa at Champéry’s National Hotel. You can also take a swim in Champéry’s indoor and outdoor swimming pools.
