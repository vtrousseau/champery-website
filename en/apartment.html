THE APARTMENT

Located at the Chemin des Grands-Champs in Champéry, this cosy apartment on the 3rd floor of a 4-storey building offers a splendid view to the Dents du Midi and the Dents Blanche, some of the region’s most spectacular peaks. With its ideal location, the apartment enjoys a maximum amount of sunshine throughout the day. This well laid-out and fully refurbished apartment includes:

  • 1 hall with wardrobes
  • 1 “parents” bed room with 2 twin beds
  • 1 “children” bedroom with 2 pullout beds (cot available on demand)
  • 1 bathroom with shower
  • 1 separate toilet
  • 1 open kitchen
  • 1 living room/dining room equipped with TV and free WIFI
  • 1 superb spacious L-shaped balcony facing the Dents du Midi (orientation south/west)

LOCATION

This apartment enjoys a great location. It is situated a short walk from Champéry village center, the train station, the cable-car to the Portes du Soleil ski area and the sports center.

It is accessed via the Route de la Fin, then through the Chemin des Grands-Champs. Champéry village is easily accessible and benefits from quality infrastructures, linking it to the Swiss and European transport network. By car, you can reach:

  • Geneva (airport) in 1.5 hour
  • Lausanne in 45 minutes
  • Montreux in 30 minutes

What’s more, Champéry is easily accessible by train, which means you can directly come from the airport without the need for a car.
