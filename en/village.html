CHAMPÉRY, AUTHENTICITY AT THE HEART OF THE SWISS ALPS

VILLAGE

Nestled at the end of the Illiez valley, the charming village of Champéry has retained its authenticity while being equipped with the most modern sports facilities.

SPORT FACILITIES

Indoor and outdoor pools, climbing wall, tennis courts, skating rink, curling rink, fitness center, Via Ferrata… there’s plenty to do for adults and children!

HIKING

Numerous restaurants and mountain bars accessible by foot or by car, pedestrian circuits all levels, mountain bike circuits.
